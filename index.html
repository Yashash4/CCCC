Welcome to Creative Creations Clothing Company

Providing best service in apparel & home furnishing since 2017.

Company Profile

Creative Creations Clothing Company was established in 2017 with a vision to provide best service. We have considerable experience in sourcing and manufacturing of apparel and home furnishing.

Our manufacturing facilities are located near Bangalore with an average capacity of more than 50,000 pcs per month. Our associates/partners have complete infrastructure—from dyeing, processing, fabric finishing, printing, to embroidery. These state-of-the-art equipments ensure the manufacture and supply of best quality products in the shortest possible time, catering to a range of products including men's wear, women's wear, and kids wear.

We manufacture apparel with high quality in buyer-specified timelines, covering the whole spectrum from sourcing and supplier qualification to price negotiations, production monitoring, and inspections.

Our Capabilities

Merchandising

Our sourcing staff is among the finest in India. With customer satisfaction as our prime priority, we handle market research, vendor appraisals, effective costing, price negotiations, and provide regular updates on production status—from sampling to shipment.

Fabrics

Quality fabric is key to successful production. We source the best fabrics, test them for color fastness and strength, and use azo-free dyes. A fabric swatch booklet is available on request.

Development and Sampling

Our design team and merchandisers collaborate to develop innovative samples. Indian garments are renowned for their exquisite embroidery, whether hand, computer, or machine done. Sampling typically takes 2-3 weeks from inquiry.

Quality Assurance

Our Quality Control team, experienced in AQL standards, conducts sample appraisals, factory evaluations, identifies production issues, and ensures accurate record-keeping.

Procurement

Proper sourcing is vital. We select prime vendors based on financial strength, technical abilities, facility capabilities, and proven track records, ensuring inquiries align with our network’s capabilities.
